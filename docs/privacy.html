At Parity, we believe that balance extends to how we handle your data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you join our waitlist. Please note that Parity is an independent platform and is not officially affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by the University of North Carolina.

1. Information We Collect

When you sign up for the Parity waitlist, we collect the following personal information:

  • University Email: We require an @unc.edu email address to verify your status as a current student.
  • Phone Number: Used to notify you via SMS when a spot opens up in the app. Standard message and data rates may apply.
  • Gender Identity: Collected to ensure the platform remains balanced and to facilitate the "parity" logic of our entry system.
  • Dating Preferences: To ensure we are matching you into a pool of active and relevant users.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To manage and organize the waitlist and application.
  • To verify student eligibility.
  • To calculate real-time gender balance (parity) to determine entry timing.
  • To communicate important updates regarding your waitlist status.

3. Data Storage, Retention & Security

We take the security of your information seriously. Your data is stored securely using industry-standard cloud database hosting. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We will retain your data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, remember that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

4. Third-Party Services

We utilize third-party infrastructure to host our database, process analytics, and facilitate communication (such as SMS and email routing). These providers are authorized to process your data only as necessary to keep the platform functioning.

5. Cookies and Tracking

Our waitlist website may use cookies or similar basic tracking technologies to maintain your session securely, prevent spam, and understand basic site traffic. We do not use cross-site tracking cookies for advertising.

6. Age Requirements

Parity is intended only for individuals who are 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under 18. If we become aware that we have collected data from an underage user, we will delete it immediately.

7. Your Rights

Under applicable privacy laws, you have the right to access, correct, or request the total deletion of your personal data from our waitlist. To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@parity.love. You can also opt-out of SMS notifications at any time by replying 'STOP' to any message we send.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting the revised policy and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us at support@parity.love.
